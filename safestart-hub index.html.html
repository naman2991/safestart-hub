1. Verified Housing Support from Universities

What: University-hosted housing listings that are pre-verified and secure.

Why: Many students fall for scams on public rental platforms.

  • Offer rental agreement checks before signing

2. Tenant Rights Awareness

What: Orientation sessions and guides that inform students of tenant law.

Why: Legal literacy prevents exploitation.

  • Host multilingual induction workshops
  • Distribute visual guides and legal websites

3. Legal & Emergency Help

What: Fast access to support networks like Red Cross, Ask Izzy, and Tenancy WA.

Why: Students need help when things go wrong‚Äîlike being locked out or threatened.

  • Include services in student portals
  • Promote legal helplines and support maps

4. Peer Mentorship and Forums

What: Peer-to-peer housing guidance and student-run Facebook groups.

Why: Real stories from real students help more than generic info.

  • Create verified housing forums
  • Senior students host Q&A webinars
  • Anonymous landlord reviews
